# Fck AI Slop

Move away from AI codebase, entirely. I want to own authentic craft.

## Backstory

I have to admit this. I have always wanted a personal page for customized knowledge base management. AI did help me achieve [that](https://huydo862003.github.io). AI did help me complete projects faster. I hopped on the AI train pretty late (around 2025 October), compared to other people. For work, I was pushed to use AI... Okay, I can tolerate my usages of AI for [work repos](https://github.com/holistics/dbml). But, I felt my stomach aching when I used AI for hobby projects... I'm hesitant to share them, because they are not really my craft.
